About ErosCorp

ErosCorp is a Greek perfumery company inspired by mythology and the Mediterranean tradition. Its name references Eros, the god of love — a symbol of passion, beauty and desire — elements that directly shape the brand's identity. ErosCorp stands out for its use of natural raw materials and its appreciation of artisan tradition, combined with modern production technology. Its products are designed for both men and women, with lines ranging from fresh and intense to deeply sensual fragrances.
