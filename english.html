About ErosCorp

ErosCorp is a Greek perfume company inspired by mythology and Mediterranean tradition. Its name refers to Eros, the god of love, a symbol of passion, beauty, and desire, elements that directly influence the brand's identity. ErosCorp stands out for its use of natural raw materials and its appreciation of artisanal tradition, combined with modern production technology. Its products are aimed at both men and women, with lines that range from fresh, intense, and sensual fragrances.he art of seduction, designed to convey elegance, personality, and presence.
